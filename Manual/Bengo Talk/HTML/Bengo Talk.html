Step 1: Finding the Target Word

The phrase on the module will be called the Generated Phrase.

As said on the previous page, the Generated Phrase can be found in the table below. The phrase directly to the side of the Generated Phrase in the table will be called the Target Phrase.

TBA TBA

Take the sum of the alphabetic position of the first letter in the Generated Phrase (A1Z26), the amount of batteries and ports, and add 1 to it. Call this the Target Position. Take the Target Position-th word in the Target Phrase (looping around to the start upon reaching the end of the phrase). That word is the Target Word.

Step 2: Assigning Letters to the Buttons

Sierra is a system, consisting of 10 (as of this moment) people. Their nicknames and profile pictures can be found below.

Acer Blaise Camia Ciel Hazel
Kit Mar Monika Piccolo Sage

The person controlling the system's body is called the "Fronter". To figure out who is the Fronter of the Sierra system on the module, take the sum of the amount of indicators and the last digit of the serial number, and add 1 to it. In the table, take the person at the position of the number you just got in reading order (looping around back to Acer upon going forward from Sage). That person is the Fronter of the system.

Assign the first letter of the Sierra Fronter's name to the Sierra button on the module. Assign the rest of the letters from the alphabet going clockwise from the Sierra button and forward through the alphabet (Looping back to A upon going forward from Z).

Example: the order of people in clockwise order is: GoodHood, Sierra, Kuro, _Play_. Sierra's Fronter is Monika. First, the letter M is assigned to the Sierra button, then the letter N is assigned to the Kuro button, then O - to _Play_, P - to GoodHood, and so on until the final letter L is assigned to the Kuro button.

Finally, use the buttons' assigned letters to submit the Target Word by pressing the buttons that have been each letter of the Target Word in order.

If an incorrect button was pressed, the module will strike and will NOT reset. Once the Target Word is fully submitted, the module will solve.
